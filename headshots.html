What's in a headshot?

One of the most important tools of building a personal brand is a solid headshot.

Book a free consultation call at the link below!

A great headshot is extremely versatile - whether you're ramping up for the next audition, or updating your Linkedin profile, having a glowing visual of your personality will help you give the right first impression.

My goal is simple:

To provide a great photo session and capture your best, most confident looks. Multiple outfit choices are encouraged to allow a variety of options.

My promise to you:

I'm committed to providing images you can be proud of. I'm always willing to work with you for editing adjustments, or changes in photo selection - for no extra cost.

What does a typical photo session look like?

1.

Bring your wonderful self and 2-5 outfit choices.

2.

Choose the music (if any) and smile! Or scowl...or look ridiculous - just have fun!

3.

See images in real-time and pick out your favorites after the shoot. Low-res proofs available upon request.

4.

High-res, retouched images will be sent to your inbox within 7 days. You will have full download & sharing permissions.

5.

Leave an honest review!
